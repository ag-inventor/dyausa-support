# Dyausa — Vedic Sky Map

Dyausa turns your iPhone into a window on the real night sky — with the Vedic map drawn over it: the 27 nakṣatras on their real anchor stars (yogatārā), the 12 rāśis, the grahas, Rāhu & Ketu, and the galactic centre at Mūla.

## Privacy
Location and motion are used **on your device** to draw the sky. Nothing goes to a server; city lookups go only to Apple. No account, no analytics, no ads, no tracking. Full policy: [PRIVACY.md](PRIVACY.md).
