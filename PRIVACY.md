# Dyausa — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 2026_

Dyausa ("the app") is a night-sky visualization app. Your privacy is simple: **the app runs on your device and has no server of its own.**

## What the app uses
- **Location** — used on your device to compute the sky for where you are and to label your city. Your coordinates are stored only in the app's local settings on your device.
- **Motion** — used on your device to orient the sky as you move your phone.

## What leaves your device
- **City-name lookups only.** To turn your coordinates into a city name, the app asks Apple's geocoding service; that request goes to Apple under Apple's privacy policy. Nothing else leaves your device.

## What we do NOT do
- No account or login.
- No analytics, advertising, tracking, or third-party data-collection SDKs.
- No server of ours receives your location, usage, or any personal data.

## Data retention & deletion
Your local settings (including your last location) live only on your device and are removed when you delete the app.

## Children
The app is rated 4+ and collects no personal data.
